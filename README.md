<h1 align="center">
   Koinonos
</h1>

<h4 align="center">
   "koy-no-nos"
</h4>

<p align="center">
  Koinonos is a companion tool for your AVM nodes that allow Kibisis and other compatible wallets to connect to your node and, among other things, manage your participation keys.
</p>
